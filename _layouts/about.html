What, specifically, is a generalist?

Someone needs to visualize the big picture, and then figure out how the various pieces knit together. The question of which is better has been asked many times over. I do worry that people will look at that title and assume that I don't have depth, but that’s a battle that I’m happy to fight. Being a generalist simply means that I choose to place emphasis on the value that my breadth of expertise brings.
