About me

I’m in the business of understanding what people want and then figuring out how to make it real and well-behaved. I’ve designed and developed a wide variety of printed materials, Web, mobile, IPTV and smart meter interfaces and services for clients and contexts as diverse as luxury furniture makers in central Italy to Community Health Nurses in Ghana. While it’s difficult trying to fit my experience neatly into any particular box, here are five that I relate to really well and am currently excited about:

Design Strategy & Management

  • Managing design teams and processes
  • Project management

Product Development

  • Product strategy
  • Defining requirements
  • Interfacing with development teams
  • Agile development

UX/UI Design

  • User flows
  • Customer journey mapping
  • Wireframing
  • Prototyping
  • Visual design

Design Research

  • Planning, conducting and synthesizing ethnographic research
  • Concept testing
  • Usability testing

Facilitation

  • Defining and implementing design sprints
  • Design Thinking workshops and training
  • Coaching on design processes

I currently work as an indpendent consultant, but have worked in a variety of agencies and product development companies in the past as well. In 2014, I co-founded The Cobalt Partners, a Design and Technology consulting firm in Accra where I served as Partner, Design Lead and lead of cross-disciplinary project teams, until 2017. I’ve also lectured in Design, Human-Computer Interaction and e-Commerce, espousing the idea of Design – treated holistically and technically – as the ultimate problem-solving pursuit. I also speak and run workshops on Design Thinking and design for inclusion.

I have a Bachelor’s in Computer Science (concentration in Digital Media Design) from the University of Pennsylvania and a Master’s in Product-Service Systems Design from the Politecnico di Milano.

At the moment, I’m looking for opportunities working remotely in UX/UI Design, Product Development and Design Management. If you have a role or a project that you think I might be able to help with, do give me a shout.
