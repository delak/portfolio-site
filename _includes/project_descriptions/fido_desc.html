Fido is a micro-loan company based in Ghana. Their unique model is based on a smartphone application through which they accept and processes all requests for loans through their own mobile application. We were asked to research, redesign and test that application to increase the speed and completion rate of the process by improving usability.

Of paramount concern was how Fido's primary target market — often low-literate and unfamiliar with smartphones — might be able to use the app with minimal intervention.
