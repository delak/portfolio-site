Community Health
Nurses in the field description:

A mobile app designed to give Community Health Nurses within the Ghana Health Service — focused on maternal, newborn and child health outcomes in rural areas — valuable information, training and motivation. Even though research, content and an initial UI had already been developed, the Grameen Foundation asked me to come up with a new User Interface that was more usable and attractive.

intro:

Designed a new UI for a mobile app designed to give Community Health Nurses — focused on maternal, newborn and child health outcomes in rural areas — valuable information, training and motivation.

brief: |
  • Redesigned interface to be more attractive and usable
  • Improved information architecture and navigational clarity
  • Surfaced useful tips by making them more discoverable

As a way of simplifying user flows, certain pieces of information were re-deployed as modal windows instead of entirely new pages. This allowed users to access certain information without having to leave a particular screen.
