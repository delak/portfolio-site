beta unopuntozero is an office furniture system designed by Pierandrei Associati for Italian manufacturer Tecno. It was designed from the start as a product-service system specifically for creative offices.

beta won several design awards, including the Red Dot Design Award, the ADI Design Index and the GOOD Design Award.

brief: |
  • Brand & communication strategy
  • Logo & brand design
  • Editorial design of 240-page beta unopuntozero concept book
  • Sustainable product-service system
---

Branding and communication had to be executed in lockstep with the philosophy of the product itself. We defined a comprehensive strategy in which we defined target users, design language, and a brand evolution for the client company, Tecno. This also included a set of design principles derived from the extensive research and product design work already done by Pierandrei Associati, and functioned as a design brief under which we could streamline all of the design work across the beta unopuntozero product-service system.

The concept book: reshape. adapt. evolve.

A 270-page concept book expounding on beta as a philosophy and Pierandrei Associati's design process, from conceptualization to research to implementation. It was used as a sales and marketing tool, and as an accompaniment to the product itself.

beta unopuntozero website

Developed in Flash®, the entire site was designed onto one expansive white canvas.
